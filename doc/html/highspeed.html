Many of the functions and algorithms of the IVT are available as highend optimized implementations offered by the company Keyetech as part of the
Keyetech Performance Primitives (KPP). The implementations are significantly faster than other concurrence products. After installation of the KPP, IVT applications automatically load the KPP implementations dynamically and call them without having to perform any modifications.
